Sanitation

     Sanitation is the process of keeping places clean and healthy, especially by providing a sewage system and a clean water supply. Basic sanitation entails having access to facilities for the safe disposal of human waste (feces and urine), as well as the ability to maintain sanitary conditions through garbage collection, industrial/hazardous waste management, and wastewater treatment and disposal.
